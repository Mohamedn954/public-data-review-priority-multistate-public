## What `scripts/verify_multistate_numbers.py` checks

Every facility-level count in the table above marked "Yes" is independently recomputed by this script directly from `anonymized_data/`, and the script fails loudly (non-zero exit) on any mismatch. It does not and cannot verify the federal-audit or congressional-letter facts, since those are not facility-level data; those are documented with source URLs and access dates in `SOURCE_MANIFEST.md` instead.
