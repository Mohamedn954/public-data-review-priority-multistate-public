## Note on Oregon's citation basis

Every Oregon fact this manuscript relies on is cited to its own primary source (the DOJ/U.S. Attorney press releases, the Oregon DHS evaluations, the licensing portal) or is independently reproducible from this repository's own `anonymized_data/oregon/` via `scripts/verify_multistate_numbers.py`. `Hussein (2026b)` is cited only as a pointer to a fuller companion account and is not the primary support for any claim in this manuscript. This manuscript does not depend on the Oregon working paper being posted to SSRN.
